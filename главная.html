About the cafe

Welcome to Cherry Cake!

The Bakery was founded in 1991 as a bright and cozy patisserie,
in the new Olympic Penta Hotel on Olympiyskiy Avenue.
The founder and ideological inspirer of the institution,
was the famous French confectioner Jean-Pierre Glezor,
a member of the Guild of the best confectioners in the world.
The excellent quality of cakes,
pastries and pastries made the Bakery popular among,
visitors and their acquaintances,
who were recommended to try this or that product.
Subsequently, they returned,
to the confectionery already as buyers.
